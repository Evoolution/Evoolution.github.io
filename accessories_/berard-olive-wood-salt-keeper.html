Berard Olive Wood  Salt Keeper  
Beautifully hand-crafted.

Since 1892, Berard, located in the mountains of Royans, which border the northern edge of Provence, has specialized in creating true French hand-crafted artisan items for the kitchen from carefully selected olive wood.

Handcrafted in France from a single piece of the finest olive wood, this salt keeper offers original beauty and graceful curves. The unit features a rounded interior, swiveling magnetic lid, and stainless-steel hinge. Swings open easily with one hand, a nice feature when in the middle of cooking dinner.

Measures approximately 10 cm by 10 cm by 10 cm. Hand wash only and dry carefully. A light wipe of food safe mineral oil or beeswax can be used to restore the olive wood to its original lustre.
