Baklouti Green Chili  Olive Oil  
Spicy. Green. Unique.

From the Barbary Coast of North Africa, this Tunisia Baklouti pepper is rare and unique to this region.  This oil is made by crushing and mixing one pound of organic extra virgin olive oil with 1.6 pounds of fresh whole green Baklouti chilies.  Baklouti adds a new and surprising dimension to salsas and chili of all stripes.  This is the only example of this fruity and spicy green chili olive oil in the world. Truly a unique artisan product with hundreds of applications.

Try this flavourful oil on pizza, or slice ripe tomatoes in quarters, marinate in Baklouti Green Chili Oil, sprinkle with salt and grill.
