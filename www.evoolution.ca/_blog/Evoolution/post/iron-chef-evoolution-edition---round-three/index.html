Iron Chef: Evoolution Edition - Round Three
Thursday, September 19, 2013

It’s time for the third installment of Iron Chef: Evoolution Edition! If you’ve missed the previous rounds, you will find round one here and round two here. With summer drawing to a close, it was the perfect time for “Pickles, Jams, Chutneys and Relishes”!

We laid out the fruits of our labour for Azkaa to sample…

EJ made orange-ginger marmalade with Cara Cara Orange Vanilla Balsamic.

Mark made roasted peach and corn salsa with Ripe Peach Balsamic.

Darren made pineapple, prickly pear and strawberry jam with Golden Pineapple Balsamic.

Teagan made Roseberry™ jam (rosemary-blueberry) with Wild Blueberry Balsamic.

Curtis made tomato-corn relish with Red Apple Balsamic.

Rachel W. made blackberry, apple and cinnamon chutney with Blackberry Ginger Balsamic.

Colin made peach and tomato salsa with Jalapeño Balsamic.

Rachel P. made cilantro-coconut chutney with Coconut Balsamic.

And just for fun, Azkaa made a roasted beet and sesame blend with Sicilian Lemon Balsamic.

We passed around tortilla chips and crackers while Azkaa went about making her decision. Teagan's Roseberry™ jam was crowned the winner! She will be judging the next round, in which we'll be shaking things up a bit, so stay tuned!
