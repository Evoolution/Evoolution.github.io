Alfoos Mango  White Balsamic Vinegar  
Sweet. Tart. Fruity.

The Alfoos Mango cultivar is considered by many to be one of the best in terms of its sweetness, richness and flavour. It grows in India and is prized for its creamy, floral notes and sweet juicy flesh. Fantastic in salsas, ceviche and fruit salads for a sweet-tart twist. Use to baste while grilling chicken, pork or shrimp.
