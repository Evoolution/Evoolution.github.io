Berard Olive Wood  Cheese Board  
Beautifully hand-crafted.

Since 1892, Berard, located in the mountains of Royans, which border the northern edge of Provence, has specialized in creating true French hand-crafted artisan items for the kitchen from carefully selected olive wood.

Handcrafted in France from a single piece of the finest olive wood, this cutting board is the perfect size and shape for serving cheeses. A beautiful piece to display the lovely grain and colour of olive wood.

Measures approximately 29 cm by 14.5 cm. Hand wash only and dry carefully. A light wipe of food safe mineral oil or beeswax can be used to restore the olive wood to its original lustre.
