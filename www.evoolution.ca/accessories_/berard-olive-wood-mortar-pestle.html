Berard Olive Wood  Mortar & Pestle  
Beautifully hand-crafted.

Since 1892, Berard, located in the mountains of Royans, which border the northern edge of Provence, has specialized in creating true French hand-crafted artisan items for the kitchen from carefully selected olive wood.

Handcrafted in France from a single piece of the finest olive wood, this mortar and pestle set is a work of art used to grind herbs and spices quickly and easily.

Measures approximately 14 to 15 cm in diameter. Hand wash only and dry carefully. A light wipe of food safe mineral oil or beeswax can be used to restore the olive wood to its original lustre.
