Tastings

Public Tastings  Join us for an entertaining evening of delicious small plates made using Evoolution olive oils and balsamic vinegars, and fresh Italian sodas, while learning about the world of olive oil and balsamic vinegar.

  • * Taste small plates and Italian sodas made with Evoolution oils and vinegars

  • * Explore the entertaining world of olive oil and balsamic vinegar with our expert host

  • * Take part in a sensory evaluation of our fresh extra virigin olive oil and learn why fresh is best!

  • * Discover simple and delicious recipe ideas and tips

  • * Enjoy a personal shopping experience with our helpful staff and take 15% off your purchases!

Private Tastings  A private tasting evening at Evoolution is a unique way to host staff social gatherings, office parties, meetup groups, or to celebrate special occasions.  Designed to be fun, relaxing and interactive with lively conversation with the host and each other while gathered around small plates, Italian sodas and good wine (optional).
